Easton Campus

The ACCESS Program at DeSales University has always been committed to meeting the needs of our community. As a reflection of this commitment DeSales opened an extension campus the Spring of 1988. This campus, for 20 years, has provided convenience for students from the Lehigh Valley, Pocono area, and western/central New Jersey. Our campus offers evening and weekend classes; which provide flexibility for students. Students are able to choose classes that "fit" into their busy schedules. Learn More about the Easton Campus...
