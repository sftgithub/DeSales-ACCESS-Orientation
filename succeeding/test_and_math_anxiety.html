Test Anxiety

Anxiety at the thought of taking an exam is normal, and beneficial to some degree. It helps to bring focus to the task at hand. Anxiety becomes a problem when it interferes with the ability to prepare for or perform that task.

Math Anxiety

Then, there’s math anxiety. Recent research indicates the very part of the brain required for performing mathematical functions is impaired during periods of anxiety and stress. This serves to make learning math even more of a challenge leading to greater anxiousness on the part of the student.
