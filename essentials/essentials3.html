Application of Prior Credits

Students bring a diversity of learning experiences with them to DeSales University.

Often, this is in the form of transfer credits from other regionally-accredited colleges and universities.

However, many adult students have other less-formal learning experiences from which they have gained valuable knowledge and skills. In recognition of this, the ACCESS Program offers students the opportunity to earn credit through the demonstration of college-level learning using a number of assessment methodologies.
